\subsection{documentclass options}
The document class used in this document is
\code{\href{https://ctan.org/pkg/scrbook}{scrbook}} which belongs to the
KOMA-Script bundle. Some of its \code{options} are described
\href{http://www.pirbot.com/mirrors/ctan/macros/latex/contrib/koma-script/doc/scrguide.pdf#section.2.4}
{here}.
\begin{sscodeenv_default}{%
    style=tst-listing-tex,%
    label=lbl_docclass_options,%
    caption=\code{\textbackslash documentclass} options of this document}
\documentclass[
    a4paper,
    appendixprefix=on,
    BCOR=10mm,
    chapterprefix=on,
    DIV=calc,
    english,
    fontsize=10pt,
    headsepline=on,
    twoside=off]{scrbook}
\end{sscodeenv_default}
